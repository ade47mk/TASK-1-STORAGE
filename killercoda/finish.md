## 🎓 Key Takeaways

1. **PersistentVolumeClaims (PVC)** abstract storage details from Pods
2. **Storage Classes** enable dynamic provisioning of volumes
3. **Access Modes** control how volumes can be accessed (ReadWriteOnce, ReadWriteMany, etc.)
4. **Persistent Volumes** preserve data even when Pods are deleted
